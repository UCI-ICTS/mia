Meet Mia

Our virtual Medical Information Assistant (Mia)

A HIPAA-compliant clinical chatbot, Mia facilitates virtual conversations with patients.

Offering medical assistance is simple with Mia
1. Share Mia

Provide a link to your patients prior to their appointment.

Available for patients and clinicians in the US.

2. Intake

Via a virtual chat, Mia gathers intake information, including personal and medical history.

3. Education

Mia guides your patients through pre-appointment education from the comfort of their home.

4. Guidance

You’re notified when a patient completes a chat and will receive a clinical summary note. You're also notified of your patient’s eligibility for treatment, their interest in medical services, and which tests to consider.

5. Ordering

The order form automatically populates with patient information. Simply select the service and sign electronically.

6. Results

You’ll receive your patient’s results and can follow up with them directly. You can also choose for Mia to release results automatically, provide education, and facilitate an appointment with a specialist when appropriate.
